VARIABLES AFFECTING SAFETY PERFORMANCE

1) SCAFFOLDINGS AND WORKING PLATFORMS

1.1     Installation, operation and disassembly plan for the scaffolding are present.
1.2     Defective and worn fasteners are not used in scaffolding system.
1.3     Fastening and supporting against horizontal and vertical forces are performed properly.
1.4     Rubbish and waste material on scaffoldings and platforms are cleared not to block people to pass.
1.5     Guard rails, intermediate rails, toe boards, screens and plankings comply with the standards.
1.6     Gateways with proper system are present at scaffoldings.
1.7     Preventive measures (barrier/warning notices) are taken for incomplete/unsafe scaffolds.
1.8     Scaffolding and working platforms are controlled/inspected before use.
1.9     Sign boards are hung indicating the maximum load capacity that scaffoldings can bear at proper and visible places.
1.10  Scaffoldings and working platforms are not overloaded.
1.11  Assembly and disassembly operations are performed by experienced/trained people.
1.12  Proper personal protective equipment (PPE) are used.
